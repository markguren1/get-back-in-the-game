Q&A

Who is this for?

For professionals who were laid off, see the signs coming, or feel their role shifting as AI and robotics reshape work.

Is this just a playbook or a full toolkit?

It’s both. The Playbook provides the strategic wake-up call and reset. The embedded apps (BetterYou, BetterConnections, and AgentClarity) provide practical tools and guided practice you can use immediately.

Do I need technical or AI experience?

No. Everything is designed for non-technical professionals. You’ll learn how to work with AI productively without coding.

Will this become outdated as AI evolves?

No — it becomes more relevant. This isn’t about specific AI tools that change every few months. It’s about upgrading how you compete as the workplace changes. As AI improves, it handles more routine and technical work, which raises the baseline for everyone. What separates people is the human side of performance: judgment, trust, decision-making under pressure, and coordinating people + AI. In short: AI raises the floor. EI raises the ceiling.
